What you can expect?

Hi, I am Nicoleen a full-time photographer in Gauteng. I do outdoor photography, taking portraits, family shoots, maternity shoots, events, and also our fluffy friends. During those special moments, there are stories happening everywhere, different emotions, joy, happiness, anxiety, and little details I capture.

Photography is a language spoken by what you feel and not what you see. It's therapy and inspiration, a return ticket to a moment once forgotten, yet newly remembered. The story of no words, the capture of life's beauty. Nature shows us how to live, love, and grow alongside each other captured in a single photo. Let me be the artist of your life and my camera the paintbrush of all your memories.
